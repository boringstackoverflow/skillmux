# Code of Conduct

Skillmux aims to be a practical, respectful open-source project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent, but prioritize technical clarity.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Respect maintainers' time by providing reproducible reports and focused pull requests.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject contributions; or block participants when behavior makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
